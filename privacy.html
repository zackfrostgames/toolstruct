Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Introduction

ToolStruct ("we", "our", or "us") operates the ToolStruct website. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our services.

No Direct Data Collection

ToolStruct does not directly collect personal data such as names, email addresses, or contact information. All calculations performed on ToolStruct are processed locally on your device and are never stored on our servers or transmitted to third parties.

Your privacy is important to us. Your solar system estimates, electricity bills, and usage data remain entirely on your device and under your control.

Cookies

We may use cookies to improve your browsing experience. A cookie is a small file placed on your device's storage.

Cookies we currently use:

  • Consent Cookie: We store your cookie consent preference locally so we don't ask again unnecessarily.

Cookies we may use in the future:

  • Analytics cookies (to understand how users interact with our tools)
  • Preference cookies (to remember your settings)

You can control cookies through your browser settings. If you disable cookies, some features of our site may not work optimally.

Third-Party Services

Google AdSense (Future): We may use Google AdSense for advertising in the future. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to collect data about your interactions with our site and other sites to deliver personalized ads. Please review Google's privacy policy for more information.

Netlify (Hosting): Our website is hosted on Netlify, which may collect basic analytics data such as page views and traffic patterns. This data helps us understand site performance.

Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. ToolStruct uses HTTPS encryption for all data transmission. Since we don't collect personal data, there is minimal risk of data breach from our services.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, data subject rights under GDPR and other privacy laws have limited application to our services. However, you always have the right to:

  • Clear your browse data and cookies at any time
  • Disable cookies in your browser settings
  • Contact us with privacy concerns (see below)
